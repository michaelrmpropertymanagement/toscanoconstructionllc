About Toscano

We build like our name is on it. Because it is.

Toscano Construction was founded on a simple frustration: contractors who show up late, communicate poorly, and finish "close enough." We built the company we wished we could hire — one crew culture, one quality bar, one promise: done right, on time, every time.

Licensed & fully insured. Every trade, every site, every day.

Fixed, honest timelines. We schedule like a drawing set — to the line.

A clean site is a safe site. We leave every workday broom-ready.

One point of contact. You always know who's building your project.
